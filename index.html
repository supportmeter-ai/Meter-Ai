Why Meter AI

Understand your limits.

Meter AI replaces sudden session lockouts with live estimations and offline tracking.

Without Meter AI

  • Only shows a percentage

    Anthropic states current usage in settings but offers no metrics on remaining messages or cost equivalents.

  • No session history

    You lose records of how many prompts you've sent or sessions completed once the limits clear.

  • No analytics

    No reports on Peak rates, prompt volumes, or daily active trends.

  • No project tracking

    There are no tools to budget or cap context loads for dedicated workspaces.

  • No context transfer

    You must manually copy and paste conversation details to move them once limits trigger.

  • No yearly insights

    No summary summaries of your annual prompt activity or workflow history.

With Meter AI

  • Live inline tracking

    A lightweight tracker fits cleanly below the chat prompt, updating on every sent query.

  • Session analytics

    Logs peak rates, session histories, and counts by model, building a rich dashboard on your system.

  • AI Wrapped

    Presents an annual snapshot of your prompt volumes, working hours, and saved productivity values.

  • Context Bridge

    Copies active threads and opens ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok in one click if rate limits block you.

  • Value reports

    Displays value estimates and cost comparisons in local currency, based on subscription rates.

  • Everything stored locally

    All data is maintained locally in the browser sandbox. No telemetry leaves your machine.

PHILOSOPHY

Designed for how people actually use Claude.

Meter AI helps you understand your usage. By analyzing Claude's network responses directly within your client session, it translates complex token boundaries into plain countdown parameters and estimated message budgets.

Meter AI helps you continue conversations across providers. The Context Bridge runs locally, formatting and transferring full conversation states to ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok the moment Claude's rate limits block your developer flow.

Meter AI respects your privacy by keeping usage data local. Optional Pro license checks verify entitlement without sending conversations, prompt logs, or workspace history.
