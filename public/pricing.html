Simple, Transparent Pricing for Power Users

We believe utility tools should be affordable, transparent, and respect your privacy. Meter AI is built on a local-first architecture, meaning we do not run expensive server backends to store or parse your prompt histories. That cost saving is passed directly to you, making our pricing simple and fair.

Free Tier — ₹0

Our Free Tier offers robust capability for standard tracking:

  • Live context window progress bar injected on Claude.ai
  • Real-time token estimation and active session alerts
  • Detailed local session history and prompt count monitoring
  • Up to 2 Context Bridge™ cross-LLM conversation handoffs per day

Pro Monthly — ₹169/month

Unlock the full power of your LLM workflow for less than the price of a cup of coffee:

  • Unlimited conversation handoffs with no daily caps
  • Advanced local analytics dashboard (detailed category breakdowns)
  • Priority support desk with 24-hour developer response
  • Early access to pre-releases and browser extension builds

Pro Lifetime — ₹1,699 (One-Time)

Enjoy all current and future Pro tier features forever. A single payment secures lifetime updates, removing the friction of recurring subscriptions. Billing checkout is processed securely using Razorpay, supporting cards, UPI, and net banking.
