Privacy Center

Privacy is one of the reasons Meter AI exists. Many AI tools ask users to trust another cloud service with their conversations. Meter AI was designed differently. Most of the information needed to power the extension stays inside your browser so you remain in control of your own workflow.

Privacy Center — At a Glance

  • ✓ Your conversations are not uploaded to Meter AI.
  • ✓ Your settings and configurations remain on your device.
  • ✓ We don't sell personal data.
  • ✓ We don't build advertising or tracking profiles.
  • ✓ You control your local history and metrics data.

Our Privacy Principles

Rather than relying on legal definitions, our product decisions are guided by simple principles:

  • Local-first by design: We run analysis directly on your computer. Your files and workspace inputs stay isolated inside your browser tab.
  • Collect only what is necessary: We do not track metrics, collect performance logs, or send analytics back to our systems.
  • Explain every permission: We document exactly why the extension requests standard browser features and how they improve your workflow.
  • Never monetize personal information: We generate revenue solely through paid Pro licenses, keeping our business aligned with your privacy.

What Meter AI Uses While Running

To provide usage estimates and workflow features, Meter AI needs access to limited information that is already available inside supported AI websites:

Conversation Length

The extension checks the text length of your prompts and responses in memory to estimate active token usage, discarding the text immediately after calculation.

Workspace Projects

The name of your active Claude Project is read solely to let you organize and track usage budgets per project locally.

What Meter AI Saves

We save configuration and history metrics directly to your browser's local sandbox profile. This data stays on your machine and includes:

  • Your custom warning limits and notification settings.
  • Activity histories (message counts, local sync states).
  • Active reset timer parameters.

Data We Never Access

The extension ignores data outside your active AI workspace and never intercepts:

  • ✓ Conversation content (the raw text of your prompts or AI replies).
  • ✓ Uploaded files, codebase imports, images, or documents.
  • ✓ Your browser history, cookies, or activity outside supported AI tabs.
  • ✓ Input forms, credentials, passwords, or credit card numbers.
  • ✓ Advertising cookies or browser-level tracking IDs.

When We Communicate With Our Servers

Meter AI is designed to run locally. The only times the extension makes outbound network requests are:

  • License checking: To verify if a Pro subscription key is active.
  • Extension updates: Standard browser checks to download version updates.

Third-Party Services

We partner with a limited list of external services to verify licensing and payment operations securely:

  • Razorpay: Handles subscription payments. Payment data remains isolated on their secure site.
  • Chrome Web Store: Manages extension distribution and user installation channels.
  • Supabase: Checks the valid key list to confirm Pro subscriptions.

Your Controls & Data Retention

Your local stats, configuration states, and budgets remain on your browser tab profile. This data is saved until you choose to clear it. You can reset your configurations, clear your metrics, or delete all statistics instantly via the settings. When you uninstall the extension, the browser deletes all associated local settings automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Meter AI read my conversations?

No. The extension estimates text length in memory to calculate progress bars but never stores or transmits the text of your conversations.

Where is my information stored?

All your settings, preferences, and local stats are stored securely in your browser's isolated local profile on your own computer. None of this data is uploaded to our servers.

Can I use Meter AI without creating an account?

Yes. The free core tracking features require no signup, registration, or email verification. You can use it immediately after installation.

Can I remove everything later?

Yes. You can clear all settings, reset countdown parameters, and delete local stats history instantly using the extension's settings panel.

Does Meter AI work offline?

Yes. Because all logic and estimation code run locally on your device, the tracking features continue to function without an active internet connection.

Does Meter AI share information with advertisers?

No. We do not place trackers, build profiling cookies, or collect search analytics. We generate revenue exclusively through premium Pro subscriptions.

What happens if I uninstall?

If you uninstall the extension, the browser deletes all local settings, daily statistics, and customized configurations immediately.

Keeping This Policy Up to Date

Our privacy center details will adapt as the product evolves. When we update features, we will reflect updates on this page with an updated revision date.

Meter AI was designed around a simple belief: your conversations belong to you. Every privacy decision we make starts from that principle, and this page exists so you always know how your information is handled.
