Complete Visibility into Claude.ai Session Context

Meter AI is engineered specifically to eliminate the unpredictability of interacting with large language models. Rather than guessing when your conversation will hit a hard limit or trigger an abrupt hourly lockout, Meter AI acts as a dedicated dashboard, monitoring context limits directly in your browser sandbox.

Live Usage Progress Bar

Once installed, Meter AI injects a subtle progress indicator directly into the sidebar of Claude.ai. As you send queries and receive responses, it analyzes the token volume of your current conversation and updates your percentage of consumption in real time. This ensures you always know how close you are to limits.

Context Bridge™

When you are about to reach the context window limit, you do not need to lose your train of thought. Our proprietary Context Bridge™ feature allows you to hand off the entire conversation—including files, prompts, and system instructions—to other leading models like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok in a single click, continuing your workflow seamlessly.

Offline Analytics Dashboard

Track your session history, prompt count, token volume, and time spent on AI activities. Meter AI stores all statistics locally in your browser sandbox. It processes your data entirely on-device, offering deep analytical value reports and an annual AI Wrapped recap of your productivity levels.
