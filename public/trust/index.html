Trust & Security

Meter AI was built around a straightforward principle: your conversations belong to you. We designed this extension to run entirely on your computer so that your private documents, files, and chat sessions are processed locally and never stored on our servers.

Why Local-First Matters

Pasting proprietary codebase files, analyzing confidential spreadsheets, or discussing business strategies requires absolute data isolation. Designing a utility that relies on external database connections would compromise this isolation. Running calculations locally keeps your active workload private and protected.

What Stays on Your Device

All core settings and usage calculations remain in your local browser sandbox:

Local Activity Data

Message count history, session duration estimates, and countdown values.

Your Preferences

Custom warning thresholds, target model selections, and configuration flags.

Usage Insights

Calculated charts showing your activity history over time, kept isolated on your device.

Meter AI is designed so conversation processing and usage calculations happen locally inside your browser. We don't build features around collecting or storing your conversations on our servers.

Data We Never Access

The extension is designed to completely ignore the content of your workspaces. We do not read, transmit, or monitor:

  • Conversation content: The text of your prompts, notes, and messages.
  • Uploaded documents: Source files, database exports, images, and codebase context.
  • Browser history: Your activity, tabs, and sessions outside supported AI pages.
  • Passwords: Input fields, login credentials, and session tokens.
  • Personal information: Names, locations, and personal profiles.

Designed Around Browser Standards

Meter AI is built using the standard browser extension APIs provided by Chrome. It operates within the same security permission model used by extensions generally, and requests only the access required for its core features.

Permissions Explained

We request standard browser permissions to power the interactive parts of the utility. Here is what we request, what you gain, and how they behave in practice:

Purpose Browser Permission What happens if disabled
Save settings locally: Retain warning preferences and session limits. storage Custom thresholds and configurations reset every time you restart the browser.
Show progress on the page: Inject indicator bars and alert banners. activeTab The extension cannot display live countdowns or indicators on the page.

Payments & Licensing

After a purchase, Meter AI checks whether your license is active so premium features can be unlocked. Your conversations are never part of this verification process. All billing is handled securely through our verified payment gateway (Razorpay).

Common Questions

Does Meter AI send my conversations anywhere?

No. The extension operates locally. The content of your chat sessions never leaves your computer.

Do I need an account?

No. You can install Meter AI and use the core tracking features immediately without creating an account or registering an email.

What happens if I uninstall?

The browser completely removes all local settings, timers, and configuration files immediately upon uninstallation.

Why does it need browser permissions?

Only to store settings locally and show the progress indicator on the active AI page tab.

Can I clear everything?

Yes. You can clear your metrics history and local settings instantly via the extension's settings panel.

Where can I read the Privacy Policy?

Our detailed Privacy Policy is linked at the bottom of every page.

Trust is earned over time, not through marketing claims. If anything about Meter AI's privacy, permissions, or licensing isn't clear, we're happy to explain how it works.
