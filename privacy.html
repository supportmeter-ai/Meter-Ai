Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Meter AI does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data outside your browser. We value your privacy and believe your usage data belongs to you.

What Meter Reads

  • Usage percentages and reset timestamps that Claude.ai's own page already loads into your browser, in order to display them in a more useful format.
  • The visible text of your current conversation, solely to estimate token counts locally when live usage data isn't yet available. This text is never transmitted, logged, or stored — it is processed in memory and discarded immediately after the estimate is computed.
  • The name of your active Claude Project (if any), to let you set usage budgets per project.

What Meter Stores

Your settings (currency, notification preferences, budgets) and activity logs are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the extension or use "Reset all data" in settings.

What Meter Does NOT Do

  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts.
  • No data is ever sold, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

Meter is not affiliated with or endorsed by Anthropic.
